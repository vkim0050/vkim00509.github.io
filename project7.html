Cats In The City Baby

Dall-E AI

Project Description: This was created alongside Dall-E AI. I wanted to portray a distopian world where onyl cats and other animals survived, so my main focus was on cats. Using Dall-E for the first time was really interesting, I still am not sure how I feel about this technology in general. It was fun to experiment though.
