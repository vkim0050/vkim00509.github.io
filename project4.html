Crustecean Alien Guy

Photoshop

Project Description: I was still feeling inspired by my alphabet project, so I created this interesting alien. I wanted it to look weird and creepy, so I used a black background and made it so that the creature was similar to a parasitic fungus that I recently learned about. It takes over ants' bodies and mummifies them from the inside wholie poisoning it's brain.
